# Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Road Accident Analysis Dashboard built using Tableau. The dashboard analyzes accident patterns, identifies major causes, and evaluates the economic and healthcare impact of road accidents across different countries.

The goal of this project is to convert raw accident data into meaningful insights that can help improve road safety and support data-driven decision making.

## Project Objectives
- Analyze major causes of road accidents
- Identify high-risk age groups involved in accidents
- Compare urban and rural accident patterns
- Examine accident trends based on time of day
- Evaluate economic losses caused by road accidents
- Analyze medical costs and insurance claim accessibility

## Dashboard Features

Accident Cause Analysis
- Speeding
- Distracted Driving
- Drunk Driving
- Mechanical Failure
- Weather Conditions

Demographic Analysis
- Age group comparison
- Driver gender distribution

Time-Based Analysis
- Morning accidents
- Afternoon accidents
- Evening accidents
- Night accidents

Geographic Analysis
- Economic loss by country
- Population density impact

Medical Cost and Insurance Analysis
- Countries with higher medical treatment costs
- Faster insurance claim processing observed in Canada and the UK
- Relationship between healthcare cost and insurance systems

## Key Insights
- Driver behavior is a major cause of road accidents.
- Drivers aged 18–25 show higher accident involvement.
- Nighttime accidents slightly increase due to reduced visibility and fatigue.
- Urban areas experience more accidents due to high traffic density.
- Countries with advanced healthcare systems have higher medical costs but better insurance support.
